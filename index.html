Hello, World

I may be the first ever self-stylized "Pink Hat Hacker". I'm fascinated by the ins and outs of the web-based global community. But, rather than choosing "white hat" or "black hat", I chose to create "Pink Hat" because I am driven to make the world a better place - much like the color pink does for me.

It is my ambition to ultimately design programs, applications, and connections broadly for non-profits, and most specifically for animal welfare organizations, sanctuaries, and shelters. But first things first: I have skills to hone and develop, and these I offer to you. I am ready to dive in head-first, adapt quickly, and take charge.

I am an honors graduate of Louisiana State University, Class of 2009. I earned a Bachelor of arts in Anthropology and Women & Gender Studies. Coupled with over 15 years of retail experience and management, I have a deep understanding of what makes people tick. I also have a well-developed capacity for leadership, initiative, and communication.

I have also spend several years in various office-based positions learning to manipulate any and all equipment and software, from a multi-line phone system, to a complex electronic health records platform. I am no stranger to perserverance. A native of southeastern Louisiana, I am a Katrina survivor. I have been laid offer due to economic fluctuations more than once. I have moved myself in a car across the country twice - to Seattle, Wa and back to Atlanta, GA with my black cat and daughter in-tow.

I plan to make my permanent home back in Seattle within the next five years. I can certainly make the trek again - I know the way. I will achieve my dream of making the world a better place one terrabyte at a time. Grab your Pink Hat and follow me!
